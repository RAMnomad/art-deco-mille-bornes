The Rules

Play Sequence

You will be dealt a hand of six cards. The standard turn proceeds in a simple "draw one, play one" style. Players begin the game with an implied "Stop" active. You must play a "Roulez" or "Vehicule Prioritaire" in order to begin moving. Distance cards provide points and move the player toward the finish line. Hazard cards slow your opponent down with flat tires, accidents, stop lights, speed limits, and fuel exhaustion. Hazards may only be played while your opponent is already in motion, with the exception of speed limits. A hazard may be interrupted by an appropriate safety card. If you play a safety a second card must be drawn on your turn. Safety cards provide immunity against a corresponding hazard, and remedy cards can be played to remove an active hazard as outlined in the table below.
